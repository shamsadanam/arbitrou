What ?

Arbitrou makes digital asset management simple, accessible, and secure. We have carefully designed a platform to easily create and join Vaults- independent asset containers equipped with IBC technology enabling interchain value capture in the Cosmos ecosystem and beyond.
‍


‍
‍

Why ?

The multi-chain future is here, yet DeFi remains extremely fragmented. Recently, the Cosmos ecosystem has released the Inter Blockchain Communication (IBC) protocol, a powerful technology to connect token and data transfer between chains. We utilize the latest in IBC to offer the most advanced and accessible structured investment products in DeFi.



‍
‍

How

Liquidity can flow efficiently with the help of Vaults built on Arbitrou.
Passive investors can put their digital assets to work with little effort while experienced investors can lead the design of Vaults and quickly aggregate liquidity.

Features

  • Every single vault runs a different strategy on a different IBC-enabled chain, consolidating the control of deployed capital to vault owners.
  • Invest into all IBC yield strategies by staking into our treasury or consolidate your capital in the vault of your choice - and gain voting power
  • In the future, every $QSR holder can create their own vault by locking up their tokens and running their own strategies, as well as receive revenue from provided liquidity

Goals

  • Arbitrou will become the biggest liquidity owner in Cosmos - and let $QSR holders receive the profits
  • Aggregate yield multi + cross chain on the entire IBC enabled ecosystem
  • Enable investors of all kinds to earn successful income
  • Own & Increase substantial amount of ATOM liquidity
  • Position ourselves to be an irreplaceable part of Cosmos moving forward

Arbitrou token

Arbitrou will become the capital management chain on Cosmos, starting with inter-chain yield maximization strategies all the way to protocol owned liquidity and tailored capital solutions for $QSR holders.

Treasury

Our treasury will act as the first Cosmos Ecosystem index - By receiving revenue from every single vault (which will run strategies on all IBC + Interchain Account enabled chains in the future) we will allow users to benefit from the total growth of Tendermint & Cosmos SDK powered products in the future. Single sided staking, auto delegation to validators and dividend payout in the token of your choice are some of the benefits of being part of $QSR and our treasury.

Community Vaults

While we have a long road ahead, we want to get to a point where users are able to deploy their own vaults. Users can lock up $QSR, fork or create a strategy, allow deposits and receive revenue for their work. In addition, all vaults will be independently governed, which will allow users to deploy a DAO and control it's funds however they wish while also benefiting from the $QSR chain's value proposition - security, speed, governance and incentive alignment.
